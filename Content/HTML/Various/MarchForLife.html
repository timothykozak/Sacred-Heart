CCD Students with Chaperones
From left to right: CJ Rhonemus, Michael Hamilton, Mark Rhonemus, Stephen Brown, Austin Hamilton & Kim Curl.


On the trip to D.C. I saw many things, but the most important was how well everyone got along together and that we were all there for the same reason.
- Michael Hamilton

 I believe that the March For Life was more than just a march or a cause. It was a life changing event for me. My thoughts about abortion were that it was wrong, unless the mother was raped. But now that I have learned a whole lot about abortion and the true meaning of life on this trip, I am against it all the way. There were so many people there that I was speechless! From the chants about Obama, to the groups with the matching outfits. It was all eye opening. I would do it all again if I had too!! It was definitely an experience that I won't forget.
- Kimberly Curl

 My favorite part of the trip was the March for Life and seeing all the young kids participating in such a huge event. Also, I liked being part of the Church Mass and seeing what it is like and seeing all the people that took part in it.
- Austin Hamilton

 I was initially shocked at the rally by the high percentage of Catholics because I have always lived in an area where Catholics are the minority. Then at the March, I was touched by the spirit and enthusiasm displayed by all the groups. I was also impressed with how beautiful the Basilica was. This trip was an eye-opening experience that left a lasting impression. I would highly recommend this trip to everyone.
- Stephen Brown
