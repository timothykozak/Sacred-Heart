Mission Statement
We, the members of Sacred Heart Parish Servants of Mary, are a parish organization that strives to do the Father’s will by following the discipleship of Mary. Through ongoing prayer, both privately and communally, we seek the salvation of our own souls and the souls of those we pray for. Through the group recitation of the rosary we will unite our prayer with that of the universal church.

Meetings
The Servants of Mary meet after the 1st Saturday Mass. Immediately after Mass the Rosary is said for the group intention. Each member also has their own private intention. After the Rosary there is a short meeting. You are welcome to attend the Mass and/or the Rosary even if you are not a member.
